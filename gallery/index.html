jsFractalZoom image gallery

Click on the image to view a pre-rendered 3840x2160 image, or alternatively, click on the link to open the zoomer for that location.

The pre-rendered images contain encoded navigation information. Drop them onto a zoomer page to navigate there.

These images are standard Mandelbrot set snaps and therefore fall under Creative Commons 1.0 license. The software (jsFractalZoom) is AGPL licensed.
